## UNIT 1: Introduction to Data Science

### Long Answer Questions:

1. Define data science and discuss data science components/needs. (2023: 7.5 marks, 2021: 8 marks, 2022: 8 marks)

3. What is Data Preprocessing? Explain and discuss steps involved in data preprocessing. (2024: 7 marks, 2021: 8 marks)
4. Discuss data science process in detail with diagrams and examples. (2023: 15 marks, 2022: 15 marks)
5. Analysis vs Reporting: Discuss the differences between data analysis and data reporting. (2024: 8 marks, 2023: 7.5 marks)
6. What is statistical modeling and how is it used? Discuss important statistical techniques in data analysis. (2021: 7 marks)
7. Differentiate Artificial Intelligence & Data science with suitable diagram. (2022: 7 marks)
